Traditional Library Services


or 'Where do I get the Books?'

Borrowing Books


  • The Library Catalogue has the listing of what books are here
  • Can borrow up to 45 books at once
  • Can borrow books for term loan
  • Can renew titles online

Working in the Library


  • Sixth Floor has a dedicated study space & Meeting Room
  • Main floor has 10 bookable study rooms
  • Series of inclosed cubicles on upper floors
  • Book online via: https://studyrooms.library.brocku.ca

Printing and Photocopying


  • Have a different printer system then the rest of campus
  • Photocopiers can scan with OCR markup
  • Full Service Copy Shop on the 5th Floor

Finding Material We Don't have


  • Often you'll find a citation for something we don't have
  • You can request that item via an Inter Library Loan
  • The system that does that is called Racer
  • Free to use
  • Article requests will come to you as PDF's



Non Traditional Library Services


Some other tools that might help

Borrowing/Buying Equipment


  • Lots of miscellaneous equipment that can be borrowed
  • Laptops are the most popular
  • You can buy headphones, pens, whiteboard markers etc

Citation Management


  • Keeping track of you citations for your thesis might be challenging
  • We have access to a web based tool called RefWorks
  • RefWorks will export things in BibTex
  • There is another product called Zotero that is installed on Library computers (if you'd like to try it)

Working from home


  • Just about everything we have is available from home
  • Just start at our website first
